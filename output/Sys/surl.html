surl使用

线上短地址服务链接:surl.youmi.net gitcode:https://github.com/fs111/kurz.go

使用API

  • 生成短地址:
curl -L http://surl.youmi.net/shorten/ --data-urlencode "url=www.youmi.net"

返回

{"Key":"xx","ShortUrl":"http://surl.youmi.net/xx","LongUrl":"www.youmi.net","CreationDate":1321486517214982000,"Clicks":0}
  • 打开短地址:
curl -L http://surl.youmi.net/eIi
  • 解短地址:
curl http://sur.youmi.net/eIi+

返回

{"Key":"xx","ShortUrl":"http://sur.youmi.net/eIi","LongUrl":"www.youmi.bet","CreationDate":1321486517214982000,"Clicks":1}
